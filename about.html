Dereck Farnsworth

I have always had a passion for handyman work. I have worked for multiple maintenance companies in my lifetime. I decided that I should take my work to the next level by starting Complete Maintenance and Repair. Ever since, I have been creating great bonds with customers and have been doing great jobs.
